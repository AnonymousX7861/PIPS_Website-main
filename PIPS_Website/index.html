Welcome To Our School

Dear Parents, Guardians and Visitors,
Welcome to Pinetown Independent Primary, a place where learning is joyful, curiosity is encouraged and every child is valued. At our school, we believe that education is not only about academic achievement but also about nurturing kindness, confidence and creativity.

Our dedicated team of teachers and staff work together to create a safe, supportive and stimulating environment where each child can discover their strengths and reach their full potential. We aim to develop life-long learners who are ready to face the future with enthusiasm and resilience.

We are proud of our strong sense of community and the partnership we share with parents and families. Together, we strive to ensure that every child's journey at Pinetown Independent Primary is filled with growth, discovery and happiness.
Thank you for visiting our website. We invite you to explore, learn more about our programs and see what makes our school such a special place.

Warm regards,
N. Muhammad (Principal)

Senior Phase (Grade 7)
