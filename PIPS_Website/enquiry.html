Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Pinetown Independent Primary School special?

Pinetown Independent Primary School is committed to providing excellence in education with a focus on holistic child development. We offer small class sizes, dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and a nurturing environment where every child can thrive. Our school emphasizes academic achievement alongside character development, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

How do I register my child at the school?

Registration Process:

  1. Complete the application form (available at the school office or download from our website)
  2. Submit required documents: birth certificate, immunization records, previous school reports (if applicable)
  3. Pay the non-refundable application fee
  4. Attend a parent interview and child assessment
  5. Await confirmation of acceptance

We recommend starting the application process at least 3 months before the desired start date.

What are the age requirements for admission?

Age Requirements by Grade:

  • Grade 1: Children must turn 6 years old by June 30th of the admission year
  • Grade 2-7: Age-appropriate placement based on previous schooling and assessment

We also consider the child's social and emotional readiness for their grade level.

What are the school fees and payment options?

School Fees Structure:

  • Application Fee: R500 (non-refundable)
  • Annual School Fees: Please contact the school office for current rates
  • Additional costs may include: uniforms, textbooks, stationery, and extra-curricular activities

Payment Options: Annual payment (with discount), termly payment, or monthly payment plans available.

What are the school hours and calendar?

School Hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 7:30 AM - 2:30 PM
  • Gates open: 7:00 AM
  • Late arrivals after 8:00 AM require office check-in

School Calendar: We follow the standard South African school calendar with four terms per year. Holiday dates and special events are communicated via our notice board and parent newsletters.

What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow the CAPS (Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement) curriculum as prescribed by the Department of Basic Education. Our academic program includes:

  • English Home Language
  • Afrikaans First Additional Language
  • Mathematics
  • Life Skills (including Life Orientation, Creative Arts, and Physical Education)
  • Natural Sciences and Technology (Grades 4-7)
  • Social Sciences (Grades 4-7)
What extra-curricular activities are available?

Sports: Soccer, netball, athletics, swimming, cricket

Cultural Activities: School choir, drama club, art classes, music lessons

Academic: Mathematics olympiad, spelling bee, science fair, debating

Special Interest: Environmental club, library monitors, student council

Activities may vary by term and are subject to minimum enrollment numbers.

Is school transport available?

Yes, we offer school transport services to various areas around Pinetown. Transport fees are additional to school fees and vary by route. Please contact the school office for:

  • Available routes and stops
  • Transport fees and payment schedule
  • Pick-up and drop-off times
  • Transport application forms
