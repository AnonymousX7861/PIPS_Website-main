Vision and Mission

Vision

At Pinetown Independent Primary School we strive to embed our motto "Achieving Excellence Together" in all that we do. Our school's vision is to create an inner-city school where every child is valued and receives a high-quality education in a welcoming and safe environment.

We aspire for all our children to become confident, happy and caring individuals who achieve personal success and develop a love of learning and of life.

Our school values every child and our vision further extends to work together with parents and community members to ensure that each child feels proud of who they are and of their achievements.

Mission

Our mission is to be an outstanding and peaceful institution, which provides learning for life and addresses the changes in the educational system effectively.

In attempting to realise the above, we pledge to:

  • Ensure excellence in teaching and learning by creating an environment for positive learning.
  • Keep abreast of new developments in the curriculum in order to transfer content to our pupils and to maintain educational excellence.
  • Promote an attitude of care, tolerance, trust and respect among the learners.
  • Maintain excellent facilities and an environment of quality.
  • Maintain as low a pupil/teacher ratio as possible.
  • Promote, both individually and collectively, the professional and personal development of teachers through staff development programmes.
  • Uphold the provisions as contained in the Constitution of South Africa to particularly enhance peaceful co-existence within our school and community.
  • Equip children with skills and positive attitudes to become:
    • Successful learners where all contributions are valued.
    • Responsible citizens who are able to contribute to society with compassion, knowledge and wisdom.
    • Creative and independent thinkers who are able to solve problems and make informed decisions.
    • Reflective individuals, able to face the future as lifelong learners within an ever-changing world.

We are and will remain firmly committed to the above principles and will seek to ensure that these ideals may live within us as well as within our communities.
