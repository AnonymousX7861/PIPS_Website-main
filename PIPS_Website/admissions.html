Admissions

Welcome to Our School

Admissions to Pinetown Independent Primary School are open to all children who meet the age requirements and demonstrate a commitment to our school's values and principles. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and strive to create an inclusive learning environment.

Application Process

To apply for admission, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form available on our website.
  2. Submit the required documents, including birth certificate, previous school records, and proof of residence.
  3. Attend an interview with the school administration and a tour of the facilities.
  4. Receive an admission decision within two weeks of the interview.
